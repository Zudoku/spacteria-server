Spacteria


Scientists have found a way to shrink you so small that you can be inserted into a weird alienlike species in a special capsule.
You are sent into the alienlike creature to learn more about it. Prepare to fight for your life after the immune system of the alien starts to attack you!


1-4 player Hack and Slash-RPG with a hint of roguelike flavours.
Spacteria offers procedurally generated maps, unlimited difficulty settings and the ability to affect the way the game is developed.
